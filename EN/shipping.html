Shipping Areas

We ship to the entire Iberian Peninsula and the Balearic Islands. For shipments to the Canary Islands, please contact our customer service.

Cold Chain

All our perishable products are shipped in special packaging with coolants to ensure the cold chain is maintained throughout transportation. The packaging maintains the proper temperature for 24 hours after shipping.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number that will allow you to track your package in real time.

Delivery Hours

Deliveries are made Monday through Friday, from 9:00 to 20:00. On Saturdays we only make deliveries until 14:00. We do not deliver on Sundays or holidays.
